English - Lesson 15/11

  • Both:
    • I have both a green car and a red car;
    • I like both apples and organges;

  • Either....or
    • Per dire "questo.....o.....": I will have either an apple or an orange for lunch;
    • Either Tom or Sally has the key;
    • i don't likn either pork or beef;
    • "I don't like it either" = Non piace nemmeno a me;

  • Neither....or
    • I like neither football nor baseball
    • I like neither of them;
    • Neither do I;

  • For agreeing/disagreeing
    • So do I (= me too);
    • I don't ( = io non lo penso);
    • Neither do I;
