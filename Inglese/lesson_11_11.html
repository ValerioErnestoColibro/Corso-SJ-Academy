English - Lesson 11/11

  • Go on:
    • He went on talking for two hours and the audience started to leave;
    • After finishing his degree, he went on to become a successful engineer;
  • Mean
    • Being American means eating turkey at least twice a year;
    • He did'nt mean to ruin your computer;
  • Try
    • I tried to call but you didn't answer;
    • If you can't sleep, try counting sheep;
  • Stop
    • She stopped to smoke on her way home from work;
    • She stopped smoking last week;
  • Be used to + ing
    • Can be used in all tenses;
    • Examples: Im used to living in Italy after moving here many years ago/I'm still not used to speaking Italian at home/Before coming here, I was used to working from home as a freelancer/I think by the end of the year I will be used to driving in Palermo ;
