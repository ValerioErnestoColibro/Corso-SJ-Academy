  • Gerunds, Infinitives, base forms:
    • Base forms:
      • after modal verbs, imperative, let + object + base form. Example: My mother lets me stay out late on the weekend
      • Make + object + base form. Example: My mother makes me do my homework before playing videogames;
    • Infitives:
      • After adjectives. Example: It's nice to meet you. It's hard to learn Chinese/He went to the store to buy milk/She waited for hours to have the chanche to meet him
    • Gerunds:
      • After. Examples: Before buying the car he did some research on the Internet.
      • As the subject of a sentence. Example: Cooking is my favourite activity;
